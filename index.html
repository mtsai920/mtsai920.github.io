About

I am a software engineer/QA analyst raised in Hong Kong, now based in Massachusetts, whose passions include video games and my cat Scampi.

After I graduated from Boston University in 2018, I spent most of my career in Manual Software QA, where I worked closely with engineers and web developers. I was always interested in engineering, and took some time outside of work to learn some rudimentary HTML and PHP. I was always fascinated by the idea of creating something out of nothing.

After a shift in my career, I took the opportunity to explore web development as a possible career path by taking a three-month coding bootcamp, and I have been really enjoying the challenging field of programming. It has been so much fun arming myself with the resources and knowledge that I now have the ability to create any passion projects I wish to pursue.

I have a strong background in Software QA, and with that I have developed a keen eye for detail and maintained the mindset of always looking at content through the customer's perspective. I am passionate about code and QA, and hope to combine the two in my future.
